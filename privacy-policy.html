Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 29, 2025

Thank you for visiting TomJRobb.com ("Website"), operated by Tom J Robb ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you access our Website or engage with our services, including the FilmIQ games. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Union and United Kingdom.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Automatically Collected Data: We use Google Analytics to gather anonymized data, such as IP addresses, browser type, device information, and pages visited, to analyze Website usage and enhance user experience. This data is not personally identifiable.
  • Voluntarily Provided Data: If you contact us via our contact form or donate through Buy Me a Coffee, you may provide personal information such as your name or email address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To improve the FilmIQ games and Website functionality based on usage analytics.
  • To respond to your inquiries or process donations.
  • To serve personalized ads if we integrate advertising services (e.g., Google AdSense) in the future.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or to fulfill the purposes outlined above.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to store your theme preference (light/dark mode) and for analytics purposes. Cookies are small text files placed on your device to enhance your experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may impact site functionality. For more details, see our Cookie Policy (to be developed) or the Google Analytics Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics: For analytics (see their Privacy Policy).
  • Buy Me a Coffee: For processing donations (see their privacy policy on their website).

If we introduce advertising (e.g., Google AdSense), third parties may use cookies for personalized ads. We encourage you to review their privacy policies for more information.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no online transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you have concerns, please contact us at thomasrobb5@gmail.com.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Opt-Out: You can disable cookies or opt out of analytics tracking via your browser settings.
  • Access and Deletion: You may request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. Note that we collect minimal data, primarily through voluntary submissions.
  • GDPR Compliance: EU/UK users have additional rights, including the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date will indicate the latest revision. We recommend reviewing this page periodically. For questions or concerns, please contact us via our Contact Us page.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out to us at:
